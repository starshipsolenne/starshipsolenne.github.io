NCC-93277 • USS SOLENNE

"Sapientia incipit admirari."

Wisdom begins in wonder.

The year is 2425. Returning to space dock after a 5 year expedition in the gamma quadrant following the Dominion War, with a new ship and a new crew the USS Solenne embarks on it's new 5 year journey of exploration and diplomacy... there's only one question... will you be on board?

Star Trek: Solenne follows the continuity and events present during Star Trek: Online, including past canon content is included to an extend too, but it runs in its own timeline, so new races and lifeforms are always welcome. ST:Solenne is purely OC driven. The Solenne welcomes everyone who wants to explore and have some fun among the stars.
