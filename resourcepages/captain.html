Captain

Role & Authority

The Captain is the commanding officer of the USS Solenne, holding ultimate responsibility for the safety, mission success, and discipline of the crew. The Captain is the final authority on all shipboard matters, representing Starfleet and the Federation in all official capacities.

Biography

Captain Ariella Fawnbrook currently serves as the commanding officer of the USS Solenne, a Justiciar-Class Command Cruiser operating under the authority of Starfleet Strategic Command. Born in 2394, she entered Starfleet Academy at age 18, graduating with dual concentrations in xenomedicine and ethical command studies. Commissioned as a medical officer in 2416, she served with distinction aboard the USS Altamira and later the USS Caelestis, where her exemplary performance during high-casualty relief missions and first contact support operations marked her early as a capable leader under pressure.

While serving as Chief Medical Officer during the Kharthan Bio-Crisis (2419), Fawnbrook successfully contained an emergent neural contagion aboard an overcrowded refugee transport, earning her the Starfleet Medal of Commendation for crisis containment and medical innovation under extreme triage conditions.

In 2421, during the Tayvora IV Evacuation, she assumed temporary command of the USS Caelestis following the incapacitation of the senior bridge staff. Coordinating both evacuation and ship defense, she successfully extracted over 300 Federation civilians from an unstable tectonic region while maintaining orbital integrity under atmospheric strain. Her actions earned her a Field Promotion to Lieutenant Commander and the Grankite Order of Tactics (Merit Class).

Fawnbrook formally transferred to the command division in 2422, attending Advanced Command School at Starbase 11. Her subsequent appointment to the USS Solenne in 2424 marked one of the youngest command appointments in recent Starfleet history for a vessel of its class. She has since overseen multiple classified diplomatic convoys and joint Starfleet–Caitian Defense Force operations along the Kzin border.

Captain Fawnbrook is known throughout the Fleet for her calm demeanor, medical precision, and diplomatic sensitivity, particularly in volatile frontier sectors. As both a former healer and now a commanding officer, she embodies Starfleet’s ethos: to explore with courage, lead with compassion, and defend peace through integrity and resolve.

Known for her composure in crisis, strategic acuity, and unwavering commitment to Federation principles, Captain Fawnbrook exemplifies the ideals of Starfleet leadership. Her tenure has been marked by operational excellence, diplomatic innovation, and strong advocacy for the inclusion of underrepresented species and cultures within the Fleet. She has overseen the Solenne’s transition into a multi-mission command platform, balancing tactical readiness with scientific inquiry and diplomatic outreach.
Captain Fawnbrook continues to uphold Starfleet’s highest standards of integrity, compassion, and courage as she leads her crew in exploration, peacekeeping, and defense of the United Federation of Planets.

Leadership Philosophy

Captain Fawnbrook emphasizes transparency, collaboration, and personal accountability. She encourages open communication among senior staff and fosters a culture of respect, innovation, and continuous learning. Her command style is both approachable and decisive, ensuring mission success while maintaining crew morale.

Primary Duties

  • Command and oversee all ship operations, departments, and personnel.
  • Set mission objectives and ensure their successful completion.
  • Maintain discipline, morale, and welfare of the crew.
  • Serve as the Federation's diplomatic representative in first contact and negotiations.
  • Authorize ship movements, tactical actions, and emergency protocols.
  • Review and approve all official reports, logs, and communications.
  • Ensure compliance with Starfleet regulations, General Orders, and the Prime Directive.
  • Delegate authority to senior officers and oversee the chain of command.

Captain's Yeoman

The Captain's Yeoman is a trusted enlisted crew member or junior officer assigned to assist the Captain with administrative, logistical, and personal tasks. The Yeoman acts as a liaison between the Captain and the crew, manages schedules, coordinates communications, and ensures the Captain's directives are efficiently implemented.

  • Prepares and organizes daily briefings and reports for the Captain.
  • Handles confidential communications and correspondence.
  • Coordinates meetings, appointments, and ceremonial duties.
  • Assists with travel arrangements and personal requests.
  • Maintains records and archives for the Captain's office.
  • May serve as aide-de-camp during diplomatic or away missions.

Command Protocols

  • The Captain may relieve any officer of duty for medical, disciplinary, or operational reasons.
  • In emergencies, the Captain can override ship systems and protocols to safeguard the crew and vessel.
  • All orders issued by the Captain are binding unless countermanded by Starfleet Command.
  • The Captain is responsible for the final review of all mission logs and after-action reports.
  • Delegation of command is permitted to the Executive Officer or next in succession when required.

Traditions & Customs

  • The Captain's Ready Room is reserved for command-level briefings and private consultations.
  • Formal address is "Captain" or "Sir/Ma'am" regardless of rank (unless Fleet Captain or Commodore).
  • The Captain presides over ceremonies, promotions, and disciplinary hearings.
  • Maintains the ship's log and ceremonial records.
