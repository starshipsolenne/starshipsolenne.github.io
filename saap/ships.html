Overview

The Starfleet Anthro Alliance Program (SFAAP) roster recognizes ships that actively include anthro servicemembers in line and command assignments.

Vessels listed below have been formally noted for anthro representation within their mission crews.

Prime Timeline

USS. Solenne

A flagship-grade command cruiser recognized by SFAAP for sustained anthro service integration in command, operations, and diplomatic mission teams.

  • Registry: NCC-93277
  • Class: Justiciar-Class Command Cruiser
  • Commanding Officer: Admiral Thea Bunn
  • Construction Record: Laid down 2411, launched 2412
  • Operational Role: Strategic fleet coordination, diplomatic response, and crisis command operations
  • SFAAP Recognition Note: Maintains anthro-inclusive staffing at both line and senior leadership levels
Kelvin Timeline

USS. Kitty Hawk

A recognized Kelvin Timeline vessel where anthro command representation is centered under Captain Meng Oren.

  • Registry: NCC-1669
  • Class: Proxima-Class Experimental Heavy Cruiser (Kitty Hawk Subclass)
  • Command Officer: Captain Meng Oren
  • Status: Active Service, Experimental Operations Division
  • Commissioned: 2278
  • Operational Role: High-speed exploration and technology testbed operations
  • SFAAP Recognition Note: Noted for visible anthro command presence and mixed-species bridge leadership
